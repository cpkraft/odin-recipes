Casein Crepes

Follow this recipe to create the most delectable crepes which are high in protein and low in calories and fat.

This recipe is very easy to follow and requires very few ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1x Egg
  • 1.5oz Almond Milk
  • 2oz water
  • 20g Optimum Nutrition Vanilla Casein

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat skillet to moderate-heat.
  2. Pour 1.5oz almond milk and 2oz water into mixing container.
  3. Add egg to liquid mixture and whisk.
  4. Add 20g Casein and mix thoroughly to remove all clumps.
  5. Pour approximately half mixture into pan.
  6. Cook until golden brown on first side and flip.
  7. Cook until golden brown on second side and cooked thoroughly. Then remove from pan.
  8. Repeat steps 2-7 with the remaining mixture.
